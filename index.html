Pat's Salmon Cookies

About Us

In 1992, after months of trial and error, Pat perfected his Salmon Cookie recipe. Then in 2017 he decided to share these uniquely flavored cookies with the world. Our first location at First and Pike, inside the historic Pike Place Market, is still open to the public. And now to better meet the demand of our customers, we have opened up four more locations. Come by and grab some Salmon Cookies today!
